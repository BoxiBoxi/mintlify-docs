---
title: "Content guidelines"
description: "Understand what content is suitable for YupVid projects."
---

Use YupVid to create helpful, accurate, and publishable short videos.

## Create responsibly

- Use source content you have permission to use.
- Review AI-generated scripts before publishing.
- Do not publish misleading claims.
- Do not include private information without consent.
- Follow the rules of the platform where you publish.

## Sensitive topics

Review videos about health, finance, legal topics, politics, or safety with extra care. Add human review before publishing.

## Brand safety

Keep your final script, images, subtitles, and caption aligned with your brand standards.
