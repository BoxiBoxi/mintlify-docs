title: "Plans and billing"
description: "Learn how YupVid plans and billing work."
---

YupVid plans include a monthly credit allowance. Credits let you generate previews, images, voiceover, and rendered videos.

## Choose a plan

Pick a plan based on how often you publish:

- Use a smaller plan when you create occasional videos.
- Use a larger plan when you publish weekly or manage several channels.
- Use a team plan when multiple editors need access.

## Upgrade

Upgrade when your monthly credit usage is consistently higher than your current plan.

## Cancel

You can cancel from billing settings. Review any remaining credits and renewal date before canceling.

## Billing help

If a charge, renewal, or invoice looks wrong, collect the account email, invoice date, and plan name before contacting support.
