---
title: "Create your first video"
description: "Build your first YupVid project from source content to rendered MP4."
---

Your first video should focus on one clear topic. YupVid works best when the source has a direct point, a simple structure, and enough detail to create scenes.

## Step 1: Start a project

1. Click **Create project**.
2. Choose **Article**, **URL**, **Script**, **Idea**, or **Document**.
3. Add your source content.
4. Click **Generate preview**.

## Step 2: Review the script

Read the generated script before you render. Fix anything that is inaccurate, too long, or off brand.

## Step 3: Check the scene plan

Each scene should express one idea. Short scenes usually work better for vertical videos because viewers can scan them quickly.

## Step 4: Add production settings

Choose the voice, subtitle style, AI backgrounds, and layout. Preview the project again after major edits.

## Step 5: Render the video

Click **Render** only when the script and scenes are ready. Rendering uses credits and creates the final MP4.
