title: "Upload documents"
description: "Create videos from uploaded files."
---

Use document upload when your source content is stored in a file.

## Supported use cases

- Draft articles
- Product briefs
- Training notes
- Research summaries
- Campaign documents

## Steps

1. Click **Create project**.
2. Choose **Document**.
3. Upload the file.
4. Wait for YupVid to extract the content.
5. Review the extracted text.
6. Generate the script preview.

## Tips

- Use documents with selectable text.
- Remove private notes before uploading.
- Split very long documents into smaller topics.
