---
title: "Create from raw idea"
description: "Start a YupVid project from a short prompt or content idea."
---

Use **Idea** when you do not have a finished article or script yet.

## Write a useful idea

Include:

- Topic
- Audience
- Desired tone
- Platform
- Main takeaway

## Example

```text
Create a 60-second educational short for startup founders.
Topic: why short product demos should show the result first.
Tone: practical and direct.
Platform: LinkedIn and YouTube Shorts.
```

## Steps

1. Click **Create project**.
2. Choose **Idea**.
3. Enter your prompt.
4. Generate a script preview.
5. Edit the script before rendering.
