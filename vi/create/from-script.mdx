title: "Create from script"
description: "Create a video from a prepared script."
---

Use **Script** when you already know what the voiceover should say.

## Script format

Use short paragraphs. Put one idea in each paragraph so YupVid can create cleaner scenes.

```text
Hook: One sentence that makes the viewer care.
Scene 1: Explain the problem.
Scene 2: Show the solution.
Scene 3: Add proof or example.
CTA: Tell the viewer what to do next.
```

## Steps

1. Click **Create project**.
2. Choose **Script**.
3. Paste your script.
4. Generate scenes.
5. Edit scene text and visual direction.
6. Preview before rendering.

## Tips

- Keep most videos between 60 and 120 seconds.
- Use natural spoken language.
- Avoid long sentences that are hard to subtitle.
