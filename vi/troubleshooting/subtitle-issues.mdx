---
title: "Subtitle issues"
description: "Fix subtitle text, timing, and readability problems."
---

Subtitles should be accurate, readable, and placed where platform UI will not cover them.

## Common problems

- Lines are too long.
- Text overlaps important visuals.
- Spelling is wrong.
- Timing feels too fast.

## Fixes

1. Edit the script text first.
2. Check subtitle line breaks.
3. Choose a higher contrast style.
4. Preview on a narrow mobile-like frame.

## Platform safety

Keep subtitles away from the bottom edge when publishing to TikTok, Reels, or Shorts.
