title: "Common errors"
description: "Resolve frequent YupVid errors."
---

Use this page when you see a general error and are not sure which guide applies.

## Project will not generate

Check that your source content is not empty, too short, or blocked by import restrictions.

## Credits look wrong

Open **Credit history** and compare usage against recent previews, image generations, and renders.

## Video is too long

Shorten the script or remove repeated scenes. Most short-form videos perform better when they stay focused.

## Download is unavailable

Wait until the render status is **Complete**, then refresh the project and try again.
