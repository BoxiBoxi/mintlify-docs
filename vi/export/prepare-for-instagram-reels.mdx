title: "Prepare for Instagram Reels"
description: "Export YupVid videos for Instagram Reels."
---

Instagram Reels favors clean visuals, strong opening frames, and concise captions.

## Checklist

- Use a 9:16 MP4.
- Keep subtitles away from interface areas.
- Choose a cover frame with a clear subject.
- Keep the caption short.
- Add relevant hashtags if they fit your workflow.

## Tip

If you publish a series, keep the same subtitle and layout style across videos.
