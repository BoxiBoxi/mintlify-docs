title: "Prepare for TikTok"
description: "Export YupVid videos for TikTok publishing."
---

TikTok works best with direct hooks, readable subtitles, and vertical video framing.

## Checklist

- Use a 9:16 vertical layout.
- Keep the first three seconds clear.
- Avoid placing key text at the bottom edge.
- Use a caption that adds context.
- Review the MP4 on mobile before publishing.

## Suggested structure

1. Hook
2. Problem
3. Insight or answer
4. Example
5. Call to action
