---
title: "Team access"
description: "Manage team access for shared YupVid workflows."
---

Team access helps multiple editors create and review video projects under one workflow.

## Common roles

- Owner: Manages billing and team settings.
- Editor: Creates and edits video projects.
- Viewer: Reviews projects when available.

## Invite a team member

1. Open **Account**.
2. Go to **Team**.
3. Click **Invite member**.
4. Enter the email address.
5. Choose the role.
6. Send the invite.

## Best practice

Give each person their own login. Shared logins make billing, credit usage, and audit history harder to understand.
