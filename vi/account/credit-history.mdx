---

Credit history shows how your credits were used across previews, AI images, voiceover, and video renders.

## View credit history

1. Open **Account**.
2. Go to **Credit history**.
3. Review recent usage.
4. Open a project if you need more context.

## What to check

- Large render batches
- Repeated image generation
- Failed render attempts
- Team usage by editor

## Save credits

Preview carefully before rendering and avoid regenerating assets until the script is stable.
