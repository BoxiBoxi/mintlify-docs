---
title: "Manage profile"
description: "Update your YupVid account profile."
---

Your profile controls basic account information used across YupVid.

## Update profile details

1. Open **Account**.
2. Go to **Profile**.
3. Update your name or account details.
4. Click **Save**.

## Email address

Your email is used for login, billing, and support. If you need to change it, check whether your workspace supports email changes or contact support.

## Security tip

Use a secure email account and avoid sharing login access with other editors.
