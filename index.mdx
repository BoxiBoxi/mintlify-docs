# Quick start

You can create a video in YupVid in three ways:

- **Studio**: Best when you want full control over the script, media, and video settings.
- **Bulk Create**: Best when you want to create several videos quickly in one batch.
- **Article to Video**: Best when you already have an article URL and want YupVid to extract the content for you.

## Basic workflow

1. Log in to YupVid.
2. Choose how you want to create the video.
3. Enter a script, upload source content, or paste an article URL.
4. Add media or let YupVid generate images with AI.
5. Configure the language, video length, voice, effect, theme, watermark, and ending.
6. Render the video.
7. Manage, download, or publish the video from **Projects**.

## Where to start

If this is your first video, start with **Studio**. It gives you the most complete workflow and lets you control each step before you render the final video.

## What to read next

Read **Studio** under **Create video** if you want to create one video carefully. Read the platform guides if you want to publish videos to connected social accounts.
