---
title: "Upgrade or cancel"
description: "Change your YupVid subscription."
---

You can change your plan from billing settings.

## Upgrade

Upgrade when you need more credits, more frequent rendering, or team features.

1. Open **Account**.
2. Go to **Billing**.
3. Choose a new plan.
4. Confirm the change.

## Cancel

Cancel when you no longer need the subscription.

1. Open **Billing**.
2. Select **Cancel plan**.
3. Review the renewal date.
4. Confirm cancellation.

## Before canceling

Download any rendered MP4 files you still need.
