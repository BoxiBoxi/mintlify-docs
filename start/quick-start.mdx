Use this guide when you want to test YupVid quickly.

## Before you start

Prepare one source:

- An article or blog post
- A public URL
- A finished script
- A short idea or content brief

## Create a video

1. Open YupVid.
2. Click **Create project**.
3. Choose your source type.
4. Paste your content or upload your file.
5. Generate the script preview.
6. Review the scenes.
7. Add voiceover, subtitles, images, and layout options.
8. Click **Render** when the preview is ready.

## Best first project

Start with a short article or a 150-300 word script. This gives YupVid enough context without making the first render too long.
