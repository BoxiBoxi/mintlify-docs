# New video formats and advanced video controls

YupVid now gives you more control over how AI writes and presents your videos. This update adds three video formats, scene animation modes, and more subtitle, effect, theme, and voice speech controls in **Advanced config**.

<img src="/images/changelog_0509_00.jpg" alt="Advanced config modal with story format, scene animation, subtitle, effect, theme, and voice speech settings" />

## New video formats

You can now choose a **Story format** before generating your video. YupVid uses this choice to adjust the script style, pacing, and structure for the type of video you want to create.

| Format | Best for | How AI adjusts the script |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **News** | Timely updates, current events, and announcement-style videos | Uses a direct news angle, clear facts, and a concise reporting tone. |
| **Storytelling** | Narrative videos, personal stories, and emotional explainers | Builds a stronger beginning, middle, and ending with a more story-driven flow. |
| **Explainer** | Educational content, tutorials, and concept breakdowns | Organizes the script around clear explanations, steps, and takeaways. |

## Choose a video format

To choose a video format:

1. Open **Studio**.
2. Use the setup panel on the right side.
3. Click **Advanced**.
4. In the **Advanced config** modal, choose a value under **Story format**.
5. Review the other advanced settings.
6. Click **Apply**.

## Scene animation

**Scene animation** controls how much motion YupVid adds to each scene.

| Option | Best for | Behavior |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Automated Scenes** | **News** and **Explainer** videos | Adds full scene movement to make the video feel more dynamic. |
| **Media + Subtitles Only** | **Storytelling** videos | Keeps the focus on your media and subtitles with a simpler presentation. |

## Subtitle controls

The Advanced config modal also adds more control over subtitles:

- **Subtitle position**: Choose where subtitles appear in the video.
- **Subtitle style**: Choose the visual style used for subtitles.
- **Subtitle font**: Choose the font used to display subtitles.

## Visual and voice controls

Use the remaining advanced settings to tune the final video style:

- **Effect**: Choose the video overlay effect.
- **Theme**: Choose the text color and effect color.
- **Voice speech**: Adjust the narration speed.

## Recommended combinations

For fast-moving informational videos, use **News** or **Explainer** with **Automated Scenes**. For narrative videos, use **Storytelling** with **Media + Subtitles Only** so viewers can focus on the story, visuals, and subtitles.
