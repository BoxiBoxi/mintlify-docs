**TikTok** is intended for publishing short-form videos to TikTok.

TikTok publishing is currently waiting for approval.

## Current status

TikTok may appear in **Platforms**, but full publishing depends on the approval status of the TikTok integration.

## When TikTok is available

When TikTok publishing is fully available, the connected TikTok account can appear in:

- **Publish to accounts** in **Bulk Create**.
- **Publish to accounts** in **Article to Video**.
- **Publish to platforms** in **Projects**.

## Temporary option

Until TikTok publishing is available for your account, download the **MP4** from **Projects** and upload it manually to TikTok.
