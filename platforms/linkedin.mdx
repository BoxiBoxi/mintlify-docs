---
title: "LinkedIn"
description: "Connect a LinkedIn account so YupVid can publish videos."
---

**LinkedIn** lets YupVid publish videos to a connected personal LinkedIn account.

## Connect LinkedIn

1. Open **Platforms**.
2. Select **LinkedIn**.
3. Click **Connect**.
4. Sign in to LinkedIn.
5. Approve the permissions.

After LinkedIn is connected, the account can appear in YupVid publishing areas.

## Publish to LinkedIn

You can publish to LinkedIn from:

- **Publish to accounts** in **Bulk Create**.
- **Publish to accounts** in **Article to Video**.
- **Publish to platforms** in **Projects**.

When a video is ready, click **Upload** next to the LinkedIn account.

## Notes

- Use the LinkedIn account you want to post from.
- Check the video before uploading.
- If publishing fails, reconnect LinkedIn and try again.
