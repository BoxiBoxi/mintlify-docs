---
title: "Threads"
description: "Connect a personal Threads account so YupVid can publish videos."
---

**Threads** lets YupVid publish videos to a connected personal Threads account.

## Connect Threads

1. Open **Platforms**.
2. Select **Threads**.
3. Click **Connect**.
4. Sign in and approve the connection.

After the connection is approved, your Threads account can appear in publishing areas across YupVid.

## Publish to Threads

You can select the connected Threads account from:

- **Publish to accounts** in **Bulk Create**.
- **Publish to accounts** in **Article to Video**.
- **Publish to platforms** in **Projects**.

When the video is ready, click **Upload** next to the Threads account.

## Notes

- Connect the account you want to publish from.
- Review the video before uploading.
- If the connection stops working, reconnect Threads from **Platforms**.
