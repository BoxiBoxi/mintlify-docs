---
title: "X (Twitter)"
description: "Connect an X account so YupVid can publish videos."
---

**X (Twitter)** lets YupVid publish videos to a connected personal X account.

## Connect X

1. Open **Platforms**.
2. Select **X (Twitter)**.
3. Click **Connect**.
4. Sign in to X.
5. Approve the connection.

After X is connected, the account can appear in publishing areas across YupVid.

## Publish to X

You can select the connected X account from:

- **Publish to accounts** in **Bulk Create**.
- **Publish to accounts** in **Article to Video**.
- **Publish to platforms** in **Projects**.

When the video is ready, click **Upload** next to the X account.

## Notes

- Make sure the correct X account is connected.
- Review the video before publishing.
- If the account does not appear, reconnect X from **Platforms**.
