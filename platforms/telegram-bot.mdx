**Telegram bot** lets you use YupVid from Telegram for quick actions on your phone.

## Connect Telegram

1. Open **Platforms**.
2. Select **Telegram bot**.
3. Click **Connect**.
4. Scan the QR code shown by YupVid.
5. Open Telegram and connect to the **YupVid** bot.

## What you can do with the bot

Through the YupVid Telegram bot, you can:

- View created projects.
- Download videos to your phone quickly.
- Check your credit balance.

## When to use Telegram

Use Telegram when you want to review or download videos without opening the full web app.

## Notes

- Scan the QR code with the phone where you use Telegram.
- Make sure you connect the correct YupVid account.
- If the bot stops responding, reconnect it from **Platforms**.
