---
title: "Create from article"
description: "Turn an article into an AI short video project."
---

Use **Article** when you already have written content and want YupVid to extract the core story.

## Good article sources

- Blog posts
- News summaries
- Product updates
- Educational explainers
- Internal announcements rewritten for public content

## Steps

1. Click **Create project**.
2. Choose **Article**.
3. Paste the article text.
4. Add a short note about the target audience if needed.
5. Click **Generate preview**.
6. Review the script and scene plan.

## Tips

- Remove unrelated navigation text, ads, and author bios before generating.
- Keep the article focused on one topic.
- Shorten long quotes before rendering.
