---
title: "Article to Video"
description: "Create YupVid videos from article URLs."
---

**Article to Video** lets you create videos from article URLs. Paste a link, and YupVid will extract the article content and use it to generate a video with the settings you choose.

## When to use Article to Video

Use **Article to Video** when:

- You already have one or more article URLs.
- You want YupVid to extract the article text for you.
- You want to create videos quickly without writing scripts manually.
- You want to schedule or publish the finished videos to connected accounts.

## Add a URL

Each URL block represents one video.

1. Open **Article to Video**.
2. Paste the article link into the URL field.
3. Click **Add**.

You can click **Add URL** to create another URL block.

## How YupVid uses the article

After you add a URL, YupVid tries to extract:

- The main article content.
- The key story points.
- Images from the article when available.

YupVid then uses that content to build the video.

If you do not use the article images, or if the article does not provide useful images, YupVid can generate images with AI.

## Publish time

Use **Publish time** to choose when the video should be published.

This is useful if you want to prepare videos now and publish them later.

## Publish to accounts

Use **Publish to accounts** to select the connected accounts where YupVid should publish the finished video.

You can choose accounts from connected platforms such as Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, Threads, Instagram, LinkedIn, and X.

## Video setup

Use **Video setup** to configure:

- **Language**: The language of the video.
- **Video length**: **Short**, **Medium**, or **Full**.
- **Voice**: The narrator voice.
- **Effect**: Overlay and transition effects.
- **Theme**: The visual style and colors.

## Watermark

Use **Watermark** to add a brand or copyright text to the video.

You can turn the watermark on or off and configure its text, position, and color.

## Ending

Use **Ending** to configure the final scene.

You can add your logo, website, social handles, and a custom closing line for the voiceover and subtitle.

## Generate the video

After you add URLs and configure the video settings, click **Gen Video**.

YupVid will create videos from the URLs you added.

## Recommended workflow

1. Open **Article to Video**.
2. Paste the article URL.
3. Click **Add**.
4. Add more URLs if needed.
5. Choose **Publish time**.
6. Select **Publish to accounts**.
7. Configure **Video setup**, **Watermark**, and **Ending**.
8. Click **Gen Video**.

## Notes before generating

- Make sure the article URL is accessible.
- Review the article topic before generating.
- Choose the correct language for the video.
- If the article images are not useful, let YupVid create AI images.
- Check the selected platform accounts before publishing.
