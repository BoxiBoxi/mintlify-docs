---
title: "Commercial use"
description: "Use YupVid content for business and marketing workflows."
---

YupVid is designed for creators, marketers, and teams that need short-form video assets.

## Before commercial publishing

- Confirm you have rights to the source content.
- Review generated visuals and voiceover.
- Check factual claims.
- Follow advertising and platform rules.

## Client work

If you create videos for clients, get approval on the script and final MP4 before publishing.

## Record keeping

Keep the source content, final script, and rendered file with your campaign records.
