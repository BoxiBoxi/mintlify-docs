---
title: "Refund policy"
description: "Learn what information to prepare for refund or billing requests."
---

Refund handling can depend on your plan, billing provider, and account status.

## Before contacting support

Prepare:

- Account email
- Plan name
- Invoice date
- Charge amount
- Reason for the request

## Credit usage

If your request is related to credits, include the project name and any relevant render or generation history.

## Cancellations

Canceling a plan usually stops future renewal. Review your billing page for the current renewal date.
