---
title: "Privacy"
description: "Protect private information when using YupVid."
---

Only upload content that you are allowed to process with YupVid.

## Do not upload

- Passwords or API keys
- Private customer data
- Confidential contracts
- Sensitive personal information
- Internal documents that are not approved for video production

## Review before publishing

Check the generated script, subtitles, images, and caption for private or unintended details.

## Team workflows

Use individual team accounts when available so access and usage are easier to manage.
