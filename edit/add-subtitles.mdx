description: "Configure subtitles for short-form video platforms."
---

Subtitles help viewers understand the video when audio is muted. They also make fast social videos easier to follow.

## Add subtitles

1. Open the project.
2. Go to **Subtitles**.
3. Choose a subtitle style.
4. Check line breaks.
5. Preview the video.

## Subtitle tips

- Keep lines short.
- Avoid covering faces, products, or key visuals.
- Use high contrast.
- Fix spelling before rendering.

## Platform note

TikTok, Reels, and Shorts often crop interface areas. Keep subtitles away from the very bottom of the frame.
