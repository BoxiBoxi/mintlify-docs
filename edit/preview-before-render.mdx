---
title: "Preview before render"
description: "Review your YupVid project before using credits on a final render."
---

Preview is the last quality check before rendering. Use it to catch script, subtitle, voice, and layout issues.

## Preview checklist

- The first scene has a strong hook.
- Scene order is logical.
- Subtitles are readable.
- The selected voice matches the content.
- AI images support the scene.
- No private or inaccurate information appears.

## When to render

Render when the preview represents the final video you want. If you are unsure, keep editing.

## After preview

Go to **Render** and queue the video.
