description: "Create the final audio and MP4 output for a YupVid project."
---

Rendering creates the final video file. Render only after the script, scenes, voice, subtitles, images, and layout are ready.

## Render steps

1. Open the project.
2. Click **Render**.
3. Review the credit cost.
4. Confirm the render.
5. Wait for the render job to finish.

## Before you render

- Preview the full project.
- Check subtitle placement.
- Confirm the selected voice.
- Fix inaccurate scene text.

## After rendering

Download the MP4 or prepare the video for publishing.
