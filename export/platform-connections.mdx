---
title: "Platform connections"
description: "Use platform workflows after a YupVid video is ready."
---

Platform connections help move from rendered video to publishing faster.

## What to prepare

- Final MP4
- Post caption
- Hashtags or keywords
- Cover frame
- Publishing date

## Workflow

1. Render the video.
2. Download or select the MP4.
3. Prepare the post caption.
4. Open the publishing platform.
5. Upload and review the post.

## Note

If a direct connection is unavailable, download the MP4 and publish manually.
