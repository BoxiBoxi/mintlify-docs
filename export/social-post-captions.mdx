A good caption tells viewers why the video matters and what to do next.

## Caption formula

```text
Hook: State the main idea.
Context: Explain why it matters.
CTA: Ask the viewer to read, follow, try, or comment.
```

## Examples

- "Most product demos start too slowly. This short shows a better order."
- "Turn one article into a week of short-form posts. Here is the workflow."
- "Save this checklist before your next launch video."

## Tips

- Do not repeat the entire video script.
- Keep the first line strong.
- Match the tone of the platform.
