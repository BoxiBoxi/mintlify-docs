Captions help you publish faster and keep the social post aligned with the video.

## Export options

Depending on the project, you can use:

- Burned-in subtitles inside the MP4
- Caption text for the social post
- Subtitle files when supported

## Caption checklist

- Match the hook in the first line.
- Keep the post caption concise.
- Add a clear call to action.
- Include links only where the platform supports them.

## Platform note

Some platforms limit clickable links in captions. Put important calls to action directly in the video when needed.
